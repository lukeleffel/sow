5 Quick Tips

iPhone photography for your business.

1
Angle from above and to the side.
Think 45 degree angle, it shows depth and context without distorting the product.
2
Stay out of the light.
Keep lighting from the side or behind your subject. Never shoot with the light source behind you.
3
Adjust your settings.
Most phones can adjust exposure, aperture and focus. Tap and hold to lock AE/AF, use this!
4
Don't zoom!
Get closer to your subject instead. Digital zoom destroys resolution, move your body not the lens.
5
Move your body.
Find different angles you love and have fun. The best shot is usually the one you weren't expecting.
